Custome made kidsroom interiors

A custom-made kids’ room is a perfect blend of creativity, comfort, and functionality, designed to grow with your child. At Ellipse Interiors, we create personalized kids’ spaces that reflect their personality, interests, and imagination while ensuring safety and practicality.We start by understanding your child’s preferences, whether they love vibrant colors, playful themes, or a calm and cozy environment. Our designs incorporate fun elements such as themed walls, creative wallpapers, and colorful furniture that make the room lively and engaging. At the same time, we maintain a balanced design that remains timeless as your child grows.Furniture plays a key role in a kids’ room. We design custom beds, study tables, wardrobes, and storage units that are both functional and space-efficient. Smart storage solutions help keep toys, books, and essentials organized, creating a clutter-free and comfortable environment. Every piece is crafted with child-friendly materials, rounded edges, and durable finishes to ensure safety and long-lasting use.Lighting is thoughtfully planned to support both play and study. We use a combination of ambient lighting, task lighting for study areas, and soft night lighting to create a warm and secure atmosphere. Proper lighting enhances focus while also making the space feel welcoming.

Creative Kids Room Designs

Design a magical and functional space for your children to learn, play, and rest. We create vibrant Kids' Rooms with customized furniture, playful themes, and safe, smart storage to inspire creativity and ensure comfort as they grow.

Kids Beds & Bunks

A child's bed should be cozy, safe, and fun. Whether you need an adventurous bunk bed for siblings or a space-saving trundle bed, our custom designs prioritize high-grade, non-toxic materials and incorporate clever under-bed storage to keep toys and clothes neatly tucked away.

Study & Creative Desk

Inspire focus and creativity with an ergonomic study unit designed specifically for children. Our study desks feature ample shelving, well-lit surfaces, and accessible drawers to store books, art supplies, and devices while keeping their dedicated learning zone organized and distraction-free.

Wardrobe & Storage Units

Perfect for keeping mess at bay, our custom kids' wardrobes bring ultimate space efficiency by merging specialized clothing storage, dedicated shoe compartments, and toy niches. Designed with playful aesthetics, these multi-functional units provide easily accessible shelving so your child can learn to organize on their own.
